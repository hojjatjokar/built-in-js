They are ordered lists!

## When to use Arrays

- When you need order
- When you need fast access / insertion and removal (sort of....)

## Big O of Arrays

- Insertion - It depends on:
  - at ends of array: O(1)
  - at the beginig of array: O(n)
- Removal - It depends....
  - at ends of array: O(1)
  - at the beginig of array: O(n)
- Searching - O(N)
- Access - O(1)

## List of methods:

- push O(1)
- pop O(1)
- shift O(n)
- unshift O(n)
- concat O(n)
- slice O(n)
- splice O(n)
- sort O(n log n)
- forEach O(n)
- map O(n)
- filter O(n)
- reduce O(n)
